Philosophy

Independent, and non-custodial by principle.

I build mobile apps for the Solana Mobile ecosystem as an independent developer and publisher. No team, no VC backing, just software built the way I'd want to use it myself.

The one rule I don't bend on: self-custody. Every app I ship is built so your keys and your funds stay in your control, always. My current and first project, DYORCULT, is built entirely on that principle.
