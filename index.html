Project

DYORCULT

A non-custodial mobile app for managing your SKR holdings on Solana Mobile. Your keys, your funds, always.

DYORCULT

Coming to Solana dApp Store

DYORCULT is a mobile app that lets you use Solana Mobile's native token SKR holdings without ever giving up control of your keys or funds. Every transaction is signed by you, on your own device.

  • Non-custodial by design: ArtDYOR & DyorCult never holds your keys or funds.
  • You sign every transaction yourself, directly from your Seeker's native wallet.
  • Built for Solana Mobile and the Seeker Community. 🫡
