Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DYORCULT ("DYORCULT," "the App," "we," "us") handles information when you use the app. DYORCULT is developed and published by ArtDYOR, an independent software developer ("ArtDYOR," "I," "my"). If you have questions, contact dyorcult@gmail.com.

The short version: DYORCULT is non-custodial. I never have access to your private keys, seed phrase, or funds. You approve and sign every transaction yourself, directly in your wallet app. DYORCULT's backend only ever sees your public wallet address, never anything that could move your funds without your consent.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Wallet Information

DYORCULT connects to your Solana wallet using the Mobile Wallet Adapter standard. When you connect, the App receives your public wallet address only. We never receive, request, transmit, or store your private key, seed phrase, or any wallet credential that could authorize a transaction on your behalf.

1.2 Transaction Signatures

To verify that a request genuinely comes from your wallet (for example, when using the in-app shop feature), our server may ask your wallet to sign a short message. The signed message and its signature are sent to our backend solely to confirm you control the wallet address in question. This signature cannot be used to move funds or execute any transaction — it only proves ownership.

1.3 Order and Purchase Data

If you use DYORCULT's shop feature to purchase a gift card, we process the product selection, order amount, and an order token (a signed reference tied to your wallet address and the order, not to your identity) to create and track that order with our payment/fulfillment partner. Order tokens are generated using a server-side signing key and are not stored in a persistent order database on our end.

1.4 Locally Stored Data

DYORCULT stores a small amount of data locally on your device, associated with your connected wallet address:

  • A profile picture you optionally choose, if you use the profile photo feature (via your device's photo library).

This data stays on your device. It is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

1.5 Device Permissions

DYORCULT may request the following device permissions. Each is used strictly for the purpose described, and none result in data being sent to our servers:

  • Photo library access — to let you choose a profile picture from your device.
  • Biometric / fingerprint authentication (Face ID, fingerprint) — to let you lock and unlock the app on your own device. This is handled entirely by your device's operating system; DYORCULT never receives your biometric data.
  • Storage access — to read the image you select as a profile picture.

1.6 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, physical address, government ID, or any other personally identifying information, unless you voluntarily provide it to us directly (for example, by emailing our support address). We do not use analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, or advertising trackers in the App.

2. Third-Party Services

DYORCULT integrates with a small number of third-party services to function. Each operates under its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review:

2.1 Bitrefill

The in-app shop feature loads Bitrefill's own website inside an embedded browser view so you can browse and purchase gift cards. When you interact with Bitrefill's site, you are subject to Bitrefill's (operated by Airfill AB) own privacy policy and terms, and any data you provide there (such as reCAPTCHA verification) is governed by their policies, not ours. Separately, our backend communicates with Bitrefill's API to create and check the status of orders, sending only the order details necessary to fulfill your purchase.

2.2 Jupiter

To provide token swap quotes and execute swaps (for example, converting SKR to USDC), DYORCULT communicates directly with Jupiter's public API from your device. This communication includes your public wallet address and the parameters of the swap you are requesting. Jupiter does not receive your private key; you approve and sign any resulting transaction yourself.

2.3 Helius (Solana RPC)

DYORCULT reads on-chain data (like your token balances) through a server-side proxy that forwards a limited, allow-listed set of read-only requests to Helius, a Solana RPC infrastructure provider. Our proxy is designed so that our Helius API credentials are never exposed to your device.

3. How We Use Information

We use the limited information described above only to:

  • Verify wallet ownership for actions you initiate.
  • Create, process, and fulfill gift card orders you request.
  • Display your on-chain balances and transaction previews within the App.
  • Respond to support requests you send us directly.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We do not maintain a persistent database of your orders or wallet activity. Order verification tokens are stateless and self-contained (cryptographically signed, not stored), and expire naturally. Locally stored data (like your profile picture) remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

5. Security

DYORCULT is built so that sensitive credentials — your private key and seed phrase — never leave your device or your wallet app. Server-side secrets (API keys, signing keys) are stored server-side and are never bundled into or accessible from the App itself. That said, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

DYORCULT is not directed to children under 18. Given that the App facilitates cryptocurrency transactions, we require users to be at least 18 years old (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction, if higher). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone who does not meet this requirement.

7. Your Choices

Because DYORCULT is non-custodial and stores minimal data, most privacy controls are already in your hands:

  • You can disconnect your wallet from the App at any time.
  • You can delete your locally stored profile picture by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it.
  • You can stop using the shop feature at any time; no account or profile is required to use the rest of the App.

8. International Users

DYORCULT is available to users globally. Because we collect very limited data and store no personal profile server-side, most international data protection frameworks (such as GDPR or CCPA) have minimal application to our processing — but if you are a resident of a jurisdiction that grants you specific data rights and you believe we hold information about you, contact us at the email below and we will address your request.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or how DYORCULT handles information can be sent to dyorcult@gmail.com.
