What it does

Your SKR, your control.

DYORCULT connects directly to your own wallet on your Seeker device. Nothing moves, swaps, or spends without your signature.

Shop with SKR turns your holdings into real spending power. Browse gift cards from thousands of global brands through Bitrefill's marketplace, and at checkout DYORCULT quotes a live SKR-to-USDC swap, shows you the exact rate and a guaranteed maximum cost, and lets you approve the entire payment with a single signature, no separate swap step, no separate transfer.

Every action is prepared by DYORCULT but signed by you, inside your own wallet. DYORCULT never sees your private key or seed phrase, and has no ability to move your funds on its own. Your Profile gives you a live read on your SKR wallet balance, staked SKR, and SOL, so you always know your full position at a glance.
