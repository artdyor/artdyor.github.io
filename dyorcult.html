DYORCULT

DYORCULT is a non-custodial mobile app for managing your SKR holdings on Solana Mobile. Swap tokens, shop with your crypto, and track your wallet — all while your keys and your funds stay yours, always.

Get in touch View on GitHub

What it does

Your SKR, your control.

DYORCULT connects directly to your own wallet on your device. Nothing ever moves without your signature.

Built for the Solana Mobile ecosystem and the Seeker community, DYORCULT lets you use your SKR holdings the way they were meant to be used: swap them, spend them, and check them, without ever handing your keys to anyone.

Every action inside the app, from a token swap to a gift card purchase, is prepared by DYORCULT but signed by you, in your own wallet app, on your own device. DYORCULT never sees your private key or seed phrase, and never has the ability to move your funds on its own.
